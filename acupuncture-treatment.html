Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine

At the heart of holistic healing, acupuncture is a testament to the timeless wisdom of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM). Gideon offers a comprehensive acupuncture service that blends ancient practices with modern health needs, promoting balance, wellness, and healing.

What is Traditional Chinese Medicine?

Traditional Chinese Medicine is a holistic approach to health care that has evolved over 2500 years. It includes various treatments designed to harness the body's natural healing abilities. One of TCM's foundational texts, The Huangdi Neijing (The Yellow Emperor's Classic of Medicine), continues to influence modern practices with its profound insights into health and disease.

TCM aims to restore homeostasis—the body's equilibrium—through unique diagnostic methods and a suite of treatments, including acupuncture, moxibustion, cupping, herbal prescriptions, and guasha.

How does acupuncture work?

Acupuncture involves precisely inserting fine, sterilised needles into specific points on the body. These points are selected based on their ability to redirect and rebalance the body's qi, or life force.

By stimulating these points, acupuncture seeks to restore health by promoting the body's natural healing abilities and reestablishing balance among the individual's physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects.

What can acupuncture treat?

Acupuncture is recognised for its effectiveness in treating a wide range of conditions. It's particularly effective for:

  • Pain Management: Beneficial in reducing chronic pain, including headaches, back pain, neck pain, and arthritis.
  • Stress and Anxiety: Help to alleviate stress and promote relaxation, improving overall mental health.
  • Digestive Issues: Addresses common digestive disorders and supports gut health.
  • Women's Health: Aids in managing symptoms associated with menstrual cycles, fertility issues, and menopause.
  • Facial Treatments: Gideon is especially interested in facial acupuncture, which can rejuvenate the appearance and improve skin health.
