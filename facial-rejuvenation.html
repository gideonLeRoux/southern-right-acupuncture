Facial Rejuvenation Acupuncture

Rediscover your natural beauty and achieve a youthful glow with facial rejuvenation acupuncture. Gideon provides this holistic approach, combining ancient techniques with modern insights to promote skin health and overall well-being.

What is facial rejuvenation acupuncture?

Gideon studied face reading under the renowned expert Lillian Bridges and uses his expertise to provide personalised treatments that address both aesthetic concerns and underlying health issues.

What does a facial rejuvenation acupuncture session look like?

Gideon's facial rejuvenation acupuncture sessions are designed to be both therapeutic and relaxing. Here's what you can expect during the session:

Initial Consultation: Your first session begins with a comprehensive consultation, during which Gideon will review your medical history and discuss your skin concerns and goals. This involves careful questioning, observation, and palpation to diagnose and prescribe the most effective treatment plan.

Treatment Plan: During the initial session, a treatment plan will be laid out and agreed upon. This plan typically includes several weekly sessions followed by monthly maintenance sessions over a year to achieve long-lasting results.

Acupuncture Treatment: The treatment involves the insertion of the finest high-quality acupuncture needles into specific points on the face and neck. This process stimulates collagen production, enhances circulation, and promotes cell renewal.

Complimentary Techniques: Gideon also uses a jade roller and gentle facial massage to enhance the effects of acupuncture and provide a sublime, relaxing experience.

Lifestyle Suggestions: As part of your treatment plan, Gideon may offer lifestyle suggestions to support your skin health and overall well-being.

What are the benefits of facial rejuvenation acupuncture?

Facial rejuvenation acupuncture offers numerous benefits that go beyond cosmetic improvements:

  • Improved Firmness: Enhances the firmness of the face and neck, giving a more lifted appearance.
  • Eliminated Puffiness: Reduces puffiness around the eyes, promoting a more refreshed look.
  • Reduced Wrinkles: Diminishes fine lines and deeper wrinkles, leading to smoother skin.
  • Increased Collagen Production: Stimulates collagen production and cell renewal, improving skin texture and elasticity.
  • Enhanced Complexion: Improves overall complexion and circulation, resulting in a healthy glow.
  • Stress Reduction: Reduces signs of stress in the face and promotes relaxation.
  • Natural Beauty: Brings out your natural beauty by balancing and rejuvenating your facial features.
  • Slowed Ageing Process: This slows down the ageing process, helping you maintain a youthful appearance.
  • Improved General Well-being: Enhances overall health and energy levels, contributing to better concentration and vitality.

What are the risks?

Acupuncture for facial rejuvenation is generally safe when performed by a qualified practitioner like Gideon le Roux. Potential risks are minimal but can include slight bruising or redness at the needle injection sites, which typically resolve quickly. As with any acupuncture treatment, sterile, single-use needles are essential to prevent any risk of infection.
